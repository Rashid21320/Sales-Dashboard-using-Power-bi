# Sales-Dashboard-using-Power-bi

## Objectives of the Dashboard

  . The main objectives of this Power BI project are:

  . To provide a centralized view of total sales performance
 
  . To analyze sales and profit month-over-month
 
  . To identify top-performing products
 
  . To compare sales across countries and customer segments
 
  . To analyze units sold and product contribution percentage
 
  . To identify key customer revenue generators
 
   .To study yearly and monthly sales trends

## Dataset Description
The dashboard uses the following columns from the dataset:

 . Sales – Total sales amount

 . Profit – Profit generated from sales

 . Units Sold – Total number of units sold

 . Country – Country-wise sales distribution

 . Year & Month – For time-series analysis

 . Product Name – Category-wise sales and units sold

 . Customer ID – For customer-level performance

 . Segment – Customer segment (Government, Small Business, Enterprise)

##Dashboard Components
Your Power BI dashboard contains:
1️⃣ Sales & Profit by Month
Bar chart showing month-wise sales vs. profit
Helps identify seasonal sales trends
2️⃣ Country-wise Sales
Tree map showing sales contribution from each country
Canada, England, and France included
3️⃣ Sales by Product (%)
Donut chart showing product category-wise performance
4️⃣ Units Sold by Product (%)
Donut chart showing category contribution in unit sales
5️⃣ Sales Generated by Customer
Horizontal bar chart showing top revenue-generating customers
6️⃣ Sales Trend Month-over-Month
Line chart analyzing trends from Jan 2023 to mid-2024
7️⃣ Sales by Segment
Treemap showing segment contributions:
Government, Small Business, Enterprise
