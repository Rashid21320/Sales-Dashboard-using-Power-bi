# Sales-Dashboard-using-Power-bi

## Objectives of the Dashboard

The main objectives of this Power BI project are:
### To provide a centralized view of total sales performance
### To analyze sales and profit month-over-month
 To identify top-performing products
 To compare sales across countries and customer segments
 To analyze units sold and product contribution percentage
 To identify key customer revenue generators
 To study yearly and monthly sales trends
